Privacy Policy for SpinChess

Effective Date: November 5, 2025

SpinChess is a chess teaching tool designed to help students learn chess piece movements through randomized piece selection.

Information Collection and Use

SpinChess does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game statistics and data remain locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process certain information automatically, including:

  • Advertising identifier
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Approximate location (for ad targeting)
  • App usage and interaction data

For more information about how Google collects and uses data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

SpinChess offers an ad-free version available as a one-time in-app purchase. All payments are processed securely through Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

SpinChess is designed as an educational tool and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is intended for use under adult supervision. Parents and guardians should monitor their children's use of the app.

Data Security

Since SpinChess does not collect or transmit personal data, all information remains secure on your local device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page.
